The Jean-Perry-Vosmer Scale(The Neek Scale)

What does it mean to be a neek?

A neek(nerd geek), defined by AI Overview Google, is someone who is considered socially awkward, overly focused on academics, and/or interested in niche subjects.

There are other definitions for neek, but this is the one that fits the way we view a neeky person.

We therefore reject Collins Dictionary’s definition of neek, which considers it to be a derogatory noun, used to describe a dull or unpopular person.
